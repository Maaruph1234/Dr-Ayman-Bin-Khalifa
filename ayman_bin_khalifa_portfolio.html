Who I Am

Visionary. Builder. Leader.

#1
Forbes
MENA 2024

Dr. Ayman Bin Khalifa is a distinguished executive and investment strategist who serves as Group CEO of Arab Developers Holding — one of Egypt's most dynamic publicly listed real estate companies. Educated at Misr University for Science & Technology, Dr. Khalifa has spent over 14 years mastering the art of cross-border investment across Egypt, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Jordan and Morocco.

Under his leadership, Arab Developers Holding has achieved EGP 3.788 billion in sales across nine months, completed a landmark EGP 1 billion capital increase on the Egyptian Stock Exchange, and developed 2.4 million square meters of residential and commercial space — earning him recognition among Forbes Middle East's most impactful real estate leaders for 2024.
